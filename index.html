Moving to California...

Getting set up.

It was not I moving permamently to Los Angelas, but I was assisting my brother in this daunting move across the United States.
We started out having to get his life packed into a Subaru WRX, a lot harder than we had initially anticipated.
Took a lot of compacting along with tears, from having to leave things behind, for my brother to make it work.

Moving right along we had planned to stop off at a few well known locations:

  • Petrified Forest, AZ
  • Grand Canyon, NV
  • Las Vegas, NV
  • Los Angeles, CA
These were all locations him and I chose to pick based on how many excess miles we would have to drive along with how bad we wanted to see some great sites.

Petrified Forest

The name of this place in itself has me enthralled, what was waiting for me in the Forest
was incredible. Large stones that took the form of wood, bark, and branches. Petrified wood is
created when the organic materials of wood are replaced with minerals such as opal or quartz.
Incredible sight to seean entire forest made of rocks.

Grand Canyon

I have seen pictures with pictures on top of pictures of the Grand Canyon,
of the incredible views of the massive canyon and its grandeur. I will have
to say though, none of them compare in the slightest to being present there and
witnessing that splendor in person. It was January when my brother had decided to
do the actual move so all of the upper part of the canyon was snow covered.

Las Vegas

After being able to see the breath-taking Grand Canyon, we had planned to drive through Las Vegas.
While driving there it began to get dark, we started to get tired, and thought we were seeing things.
Driving through the pitch black, then among the horizon we began to see feignt glow in the distance of the
famed City of Lights! This was an delightfully unexpected welcome to the city considering i've heard so
much about it. Upon coming to the city we decided to get a hotel room for the night and have an overly price
meal, both where phenomenal.

Los Angeles

Staying the night in Las Vegas had boosted our energy and motivation to move toward our final
destination, Los Angeles. There was a lot to see coming into the city and best of all, the TRAFFIC!
My brother moved into an appartment with some friends, got to do some sight seeing and see
Venice Beach! There were some incredible views in LA, even through the bustling city.
